About Us

Lockstown Training is a healthcare organisation based in the West Midlands region of England UK. We focus on providing training for healthcare professionals, businesses and the general public on key life saving techniques and general health assistance.

Our Team

Tina Titley Managing Director

Tina Titley is a Nurse Practitioner with experience consisting of more than 37 years in the health sector. She is a hardworking and competent professional who has gained the necessary skills needed to deliver high quality, customer focused and clinically effective patient care. A notable asset she has honed is her knowledge in regards to First Aid and CPR, which she has been a trainer and assessor for 24 years. It is this knowledge, which she aims to provide with Lockstown Training.

Jacqueline Timbrell Advisor

Jacqueline Timbrell has been a qualified healthcare professional for over 30 years, initially beginning her career in nursing consisting of adult and paediatric divisions then transferring to the field of health and safety. Jacqueline prides herself on delivering information that can be received by all audiences who partake in the sessions at Lockstown Training.
