CPR & Anaphylaxis


This is a two hour course aimed towards anyone who needs training in CPR, Choking and Anaphylaxis. Suitable for administrative and clinical staff in General Practise.

Course Content

  • Assess an incident and explain rationale for checking danger
  • Demonstrate how to open an airway and check for breathing
  • Demonstrate the importance of sending for help
  • Demonstrate how to deal with an unconscious casualty
  • Demonstrate the recovery positon
  • Demonstrate how to perform Cardio Pulmonary Resuscitation (CPR) on a non-breathing casualty (Adult, Child and Infant)
  • Demonstrate how to deal with a choking casualty (Adult, Child and Infant)
  • Anaphylaxis Training
  • Automated External Defibrillation (AED) training
